About Us

KICKSTART is a programme that provide opportunities to everyone through training and equip everyone with entrepreneur skills and bridging them into the 1st Telco Industry in Malaysia.

Our Mission

Inspire,empower,promote youth and opportunities seekers to achieve their financial freedom by bridging them into telco industry with skills,knowledge and resources needed

Our Vision

To Be The ONE of the Telco Players that connects everyone to achieve business and personal success and to reach a higher level Of Prosperity, Benefit Society, And unlock Our Innovation in entrepreneurship ideas.

Our Value

Our kickstart program, better begins with you, enables our people within our organization who consistently live our values and bring the purpose of building a better opportunities to other's life through their guidance, which in turn makes an impact on our partners, people and communities.
